About Cool Maths Zone

One developer's passion project to make mathematics fun and rewarding for children everywhere

Bishu Som

Founder & Solo Developer

Hello! I'm the creator behind Cool Maths Zone. What began as a side project to help my niece with math homework has grown into a platform enjoyed by thousands of students worldwide.

With 22 years of experience in web development and technology, I've combined my technical skills with a passion for making learning enjoyable. Every line of code, every game mechanic, and every math problem has been carefully crafted by me to create an engaging learning experience.

When I'm not coding new features, you'll find me reading user feedback, analyzing gameplay data to improve the experience, or creating new math challenges.

If you are interested you can try trivia puzzles on my other site www.triviaah.com !

My Mission

"To transform math anxiety into math confidence through playful learning experiences that children actually enjoy."

From Frustration to Fun

I remember struggling with math as a child. Cool Maths Zone is designed to prevent that frustration by making practice enjoyable and rewarding.

Quality Over Quantity

As a solo developer, I focus on creating fewer but more meaningful games that truly help children learn rather than just keeping them busy.

Accessible to All

I'm committed to keeping Cool Maths Zone free so every child, regardless of background, can benefit from these learning tools.

Educational Approach

Game-Based Learning

I incorporate proven game mechanics that motivate practice while maintaining educational integrity. Points, levels, and rewards make learning addictive in the best way.

Growth Mindset

Every game emphasizes that math skills can be developed through practice. Mistakes are framed as learning opportunities, not failures.

Aligned with Standards

All activities complement classroom learning and align with Common Core State Standards (CCSS) and other international math curricula.
