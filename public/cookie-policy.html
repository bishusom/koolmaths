What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide a better experience for you.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • See what cookies are stored
  • Delete individual cookies
  • Block cookies from specific sites
  • Block all cookies

To change your consent preferences on our site, click the "Cookie Settings" button in the footer.
