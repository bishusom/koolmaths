Division Strategies

1. Equal Sharing

Share equally into groups:

Example: 12 ÷ 3

Share 12 cookies 🍪 among 3 friends:

Friend 1: 🍪🍪🍪🍪

Friend 2: 🍪🍪🍪🍪

Friend 3: 🍪🍪🍪🍪

Answer: 4 each

12🍪 ÷ 3 = 4🍪 each

2. Repeated Subtraction

Subtract the divisor repeatedly:

Example: 15 ÷ 5

15 - 5 = 10 (1)

10 - 5 = 5 (2)

5 - 5 = 0 (3)

Subtracted 5 three times

Answer: 3

15➖5➖5➖5=0 → 3 times

3. Using Multiplication Facts

Think of related multiplication:

Example: 24 ÷ 6

Think: "What times 6 equals 24?"

6 × 4 = 24, so 24 ÷ 6 = 4

Answer: 4

6 × ? = 24 → 4

4. Arrays

5. Dividing by One

Special rule for dividing by one:

Example: 9 ÷ 1

Any number divided by 1 is itself

Answer: 9
